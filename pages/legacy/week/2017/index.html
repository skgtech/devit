Each year we grow bigger. This year we introduce the DEVit week. Our local communities have grown strong and are ready to celebrate the biggest technological event of the year with a full week of festivities. Each day there will be multiple meetups, gatherings and events, accompanied by our international speakers. As the conference grows so do our budgets, this allows us to host the international speakers for longer periods of time maximizing their impact on the local community and creating new, international bonds that open up new opportunities and knowledge sharing on all the levels.

  • We are Together III

    "We are Together" is an afternoon gathering where local techies come together to present their products, to listen to fresh ideas and to network. Participation is admission free and open to anyone interested into new technologies and business creation.

    The event is co-organised by SKGTech, SKGStartuppers and OK!Thess but it's made possible by all of the communities and startups participating.

  • Nodejs/.NET Synergy Meetup + 3 free tickets for DEVit Conference

    May Nodejs Meetup is back! This time with our fellow .NET folks. The upcoming Node.js meetup is a special edition cause we will have many guests joining us including the awesome Toptal team. Our meetup lineup, Asia Lindsay from Toptal will present `How Remote Work and Freelancing is Changing the World`. James Halliday (DEVit speaker) will present 'Live coding a webgl+webaudio demo from zero' and professors Alexander Chatzigeorgiou & Apostolos Ampatzoglou will share their knowledge on Technical debt.
