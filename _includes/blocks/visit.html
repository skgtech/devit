Visit

Swim in the crystal clear waters of Chalkidiki, just 40 minutes away
from the city center. Discover the history of Thessaloniki through
the numerous churches and archeological sites like
The White Tower and Artistotelous Square.

Experience

Take a gastronomy journey of Greece through the unique tastes that Thessaloniki
has to offer and immerse you self in the vibrant culture of the city.
Go for an afternoon coffee, a walk near the Waterfront or go out for drinks.
No matter your preferences, Thessaloniki's nightlife will not disappoint you.
